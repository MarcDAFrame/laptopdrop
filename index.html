The Team

About

Our Goal

To promote computer literacy through the support of ‘at risk teens’* and clubs by refurbishing/refreshing and donating old computers. This process revolves around the donation of computers from offices, individuals and school faculty to a group of trained volunteers to complete the task at hand.

*identified by school faculty and guidance.

What We Do

We wipe, refurbish, and distribute donated computers to students and clubs in need.

This process includes, inspecting, cleaning, minimal repair, and installation of Operating Systems and Third Party Software

FAQS

Q: How do I apply for a Laptop?

If you are requesting laptops for your club, please contact us via email at [info@laptopdrop.site]. If you are requesting a laptop for personal use, please contact your vice principal and/or guidance first.

Q: what is the typical process for donation?

If are donating laptops from a company, please email us at [donate@laptopdrop.site]. Before donating any laptops, please ensure the following: i)They are in proper working condition ii)They do not contain personal info/confidential data iii)The laptops are able to handle modern applications In the body of the email, please specify the number of units and the name of the organization.

Q: How do we ensure the safety of your data:

We recommend that all donated units are wiped clean or factory reset. When we first receive the laptops, the first step we take is to wipe the hard drive and install a new operating system such as Ubuntu or ChromeOS. We do NOT access any files on the hard drive before, during, or after the installation of the OS. You can rest assured that no confidential info will be compromised.

Q: Where do the laptops go?

All laptops go straight to clubs, such as the robotics club or computer science club, or to students who need them.

Q: Do students keep the laptops?

The laptops are loaned to clubs and students. They can be used throughout the school year, but they are to be returned back to the club before the end of the school year.
